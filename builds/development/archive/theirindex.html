Welcome to The Club!

The Club at Shell Point is your destination for a perfect day. Whether your preference is for a fine meal, a great round of golf, or to pamper yourself at the Salon and Spa, The Club at Shell Point has it all.

Located at Shell Point Golf Club’s 18-hole championship course, The Club at Shell Point centers around a fully equipped pro shop, climate-controlled cart barn, and locker rooms with lounge area. The Club’s two-story, 35,000-square-foot design provides a comfortable place to gather and relax, with formal and casual dining options, professional salon and spa services, fitness and aerobics center, and spacious meeting spaces available for booking, among other luxury amenities.
